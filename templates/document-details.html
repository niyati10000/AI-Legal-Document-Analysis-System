EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT THIS EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT (the "Agreement") is made and entered into this 15th day of January, 2024, by and between Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), and John Smith (the "Executive"). WHEREAS, the Company desires to employ the Executive, and the Executive desires to accept such employment, upon the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth. NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows: 1. EMPLOYMENT. The Company hereby employs the Executive, and the Executive hereby accepts employment with the Company, upon the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement. 2. POSITION AND DUTIES. 2.1 Position. The Executive shall serve as Senior Software Engineer, reporting directly to the Chief Technology Officer. 2.2 Duties. The Executive shall perform such duties and responsibilities as are customarily associated with such position and as may be assigned from time to time by the Company. 3. COMPENSATION. 3.1 Base Salary. The Company shall pay the Executive a base salary at the rate of One Hundred Twenty Thousand Dollars ($120,000) per annum, payable in accordance with the Company's standard payroll practices. 3.2 Annual Bonus. The Executive shall be eligible to receive an annual performance bonus of up to fifteen percent (15%) of base salary, based upon achievement of performance objectives established by the Company. 3.3 Signing Bonus. The Company shall pay the Executive a one-time signing bonus of Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000), payable within thirty (30) days of the commencement date. 4. BENEFITS. The Executive shall be entitled to participate in all employee benefit plans and programs generally made available to employees of the Company, including but not limited to: - Health insurance - 401(k) retirement plan with company match - Paid time off (20 days per year) - Professional development allowance 5. TERM AND TERMINATION. 5.1 Term. This Agreement shall have an initial term of one (1) year, commencing on January 15, 2024 (the "Effective Date"), and shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current term. 5.2 Termination for Cause. The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time for cause, including but not limited to material breach of this Agreement, gross negligence, or commission of a felony. 5.3 Termination without Cause. The Company may terminate this Agreement without cause upon thirty (30) days written notice to the Executive. 5.4 Resignation. The Executive may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice to the Company. 6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY. 6.1 Ownership. All inventions, discoveries, developments, improvements, and other works (whether or not patentable or copyrightable) conceived or made by the Executive during the term of employment shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company. 6.2 Assignment. The Executive hereby assigns to the Company all rights, title, and interest in and to any such intellectual property. 7. NON-COMPETE OBLIGATIONS. 7.1 Non-Competition. For a period of six (6) months following termination of employment, the Executive shall not, within a fifty (50) mile radius of any Company office, engage in any business that competes with the Company. 7.2 Non-Solicitation. The Executive shall not solicit or encourage any employee of the Company to terminate their employment. 8. CONFIDENTIALITY. The Executive shall not disclose any confidential information of the Company during or after employment. 9. ARBITRATION. Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved by binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. 10. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware. IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.

Frequently Asked Questions

What document types are supported?

LexAI supports PDF, DOCX, and TXT files up to 50MB. For scanned documents, our OCR technology automatically extracts text for analysis.

How does bias detection work?

Our AI models analyze language patterns, terminology, and historical legal data to identify potential gender, racial, or socioeconomic biases in legal texts.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all documents are encrypted at rest and in transit. PII is automatically masked, and we are compliant with GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC2 standards.

How long does analysis take?

Average processing time is 30-60 seconds per document, depending on length and complexity. You'll receive a notification when analysis is complete.

Which languages are supported?

Currently, we support English, Spanish, French, and German legal documents. More languages are coming soon.

How accurate is the AI?

Our models achieve 98.3% accuracy on legal document analysis, with continuous improvement through regular training on new legal datasets.
